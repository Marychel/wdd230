Description

Akarui mirai is a Japanese term which means bright future. It embodies the concept of making the life of young learners both Japanese and foreign childen with diverse background living in Japan especially in terms of education, manner, customs, lifestyle, language, and culture. It provides easy access that caters the basic needs for moral, psychological, secular and social aspect of the twenty first-century young learners.
