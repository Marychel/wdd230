Target Audience

Akarui Mirai is for all ages, especially the young learners and their parents. It also support goverment and non-profit organization looking for additional resources when helping children with their education. This is a free access for all.

Personas

  • Dora the Adventurer (35)

    • A mother of two kids. She is an online yoga instructor and a writer. She spends a day with her kids on weekends and makes sure to give them a wholesome adventure whenever possible, so she depends on the weather forecast, suggested safety transportation schemes, and preparations for the trips.

  • Jose Nax (22)

    • Professional Track and Field Athlete. He trains four times a week indoor/open stadium and three days in the outback. He usually depends on his mobile phone to access weather forecasts, routes and adjusts his weekly exercise routine plans and safe transportation.

Scenarios

  1. While preparing her lessons for a new student, a Japanese teacher searched the internet for teaching materials. The student is from another country and does not speak Japanese. The teaching materials she needed were found on "akaruimirai.com."

  2. An American mother wants to invite her children to live with her in Japan. She wants her children to learn basic Japanese. A video caught her attention on the Facebook page, and as soon as she watched the video, she opened the link and shared akaruimirai.com with her children.

  3. There was a student in junior high school studying Japanese. It's hard for him to understand the instructions of his Japanese teacher, so he's looking for some resources to help. A friend introduced him to akaruimirai.com, where he began learning Hiragana characters.
