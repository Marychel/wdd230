Target Audience

Akarui Mirai is for all ages, especially the young learners and their parents. It also support goverment and non-profit organization looking for additional resources when helping children learning the Japanese language. This is a free access for all and is available in four languages (English, Portuguese, Spanish and Chinese.s)

Personas

  • Mika Chavez (65)

    • A mother of two kids. She works online as a Japanese teacher. She uses the akaruimirai.com as one of her teaching materials because it is available in four languages.

  • Naoto Power (22)

    • Licensed teacher in elementary school. He is in-charge of teaching foreign students and prepares the teaching materials. He uses akaruimirai.com as a beginner's platform for teaching basic Japanese.

Scenarios

  1. While preparing her lessons for a new student, a Japanese teacher searched the internet for teaching materials. The student is from another country and does not speak Japanese. The teaching materials she needed were found on "akaruimirai.com."

  2. An American mother wants to invite her children to live with her in Japan. She wants her children to learn basic Japanese. A video caught her attention on the Facebook page, and as soon as she watched the video, she opened the link and shared akaruimirai.com with her children.

  3. There was a student in junior high school studying Japanese. It's hard for him to understand the instructions of his Japanese teacher, so he's looking for some resources to help. A friend introduced him to akaruimirai.com, where he began learning Hiragana characters.
