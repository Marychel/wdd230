Design Principles

Marychel Calip

Contrast

APPLE

https://www.apple.com/

Contrast is very evident on the Apple website. With its black color, Apple's logo exhibits extraordinary contrast that makes it stand out. The color contrast of apple products, especially the apple watch, makes the design unique and interesting for those who use Apple products.

Alignment

ADOBE

https://www.adobe.com/

The Adobe website is a great example of a wide variety of design principles. There are many factors to consider, including alignment. We can see that the text, images, and shapes are all aligned to make the whole concept of the website quite appealing.

White Space

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/

Several white spaces are visible on the website of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Throughout the website, the background color is white, as well as the text color.
